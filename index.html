Software Engineering Club at The Ohio State University _

👀 About

DEV at OSU is a student organization that aims to provide students with the opportunity to learn new tech in web dev, desktop, and other branches of programming through workshops.

We are also committed to supporting your personal software projects by providing mentorship and computing resources (free hosting and subdomains).

This club is welcoming every student regardless of their experience, major, or year at OSU.
